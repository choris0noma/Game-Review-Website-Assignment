Genshin Impact Leak Teases Major Upcoming Event

  • Genshin Impact's newest leaks are teasing the impending arrival of the game's annual Lantern Rite events in Version 5.3.
  • While no content for the update has been officially revealed, Lantern Rite is expected to bring new characters, potential skins, and more.
  • Lantern Rite looks to follow up on the landmark launch of Natlan, bringing a number of unique improvements with the game's newest region.

A new leak from Genshin Impact is hinting at the upcoming release of one of the game's most highly-anticipated annual events, teasing the window for the Lantern Rite. The massively popular RPG from HoYoverse typically features a variety of new Genshin Impact events with every update to the game, offering plenty of exclusive rewards and Primogems for players to acquire. Each patch typically includes one major event throughout its run, alongside numerous smaller combat and exploration-focused challenges. Now, one of the HoYoverse RPG's most popular events is set to make a comeback.

The Lantern Rite is one of the biggest recurring events for Genshin Impact's annual calendar, featuring a large-scale event and plenty of new content. The yearly events see players returning to Liyue for the festival, typically bringing multiple new characters from the China-themed region to the roster. HoYoverse has also often featured new skins as part of its Lantern Rite events, with 2024's Lantern Rite adding skins for Ganyu and Shenhe. The next iteration of the event is now said to be on the way, arriving a bit earlier than last year's event.

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a 2020 action role-playing video game produced by MiHoYo/HoYoverse.The game features an anime-style open world environment and an action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching. A free-to-play game monetized through gacha game mechanics, Genshin Impact is updated regularly using the games as a service model; it was originally released for Android, iOS, PlayStation 4, and Windows, followed by PlayStation 5 in 2021, with an Xbox Series X/S version coming in November 2024.
